# Impeccable — Project Design Context

_Persistent design context for joshferrara.com. Loaded by impeccable design skills before any design work._

## Design Context

### Users
The primary visitor is a **professional peer** — someone who might hire Josh, collaborate with him, or follow his product/AI work. They typically arrive from his writing, from X/social, or via referral, and they are **discerning** (designers, PMs, founders, engineers). The site is a **credibility surface** as much as a personal home: within ~15 seconds it should signal *"this is someone with serious taste and craft — worth knowing."* Secondary audiences: readers/curious followers, and Josh himself treating it as a living index of his life (writing, family, music, film, books).

### Brand Personality
**Refined & confident.** Quiet luxury — editorial restraint where every detail is deliberate. Warm, literate, and grounded (Baton Rouge; family; music). Voice is thoughtful, unhurried, precise. Three words: **refined, confident, warm.** Emotional goal: the visitor should feel they're in the hands of someone with impeccable judgment.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm **editorial / print-inspired**. Paper-toned light background (`#faf8f5`), **British Racing Green** accent (`#2d6a4f`), warm tan secondary (`#c2956a`). Type: **Playfair Display** (display), **Source Sans 3** (body), **JetBrains Mono** (sparing). Light + dark are both first-class (currently `prefers-color-scheme` only, no manual toggle).

**Existing signature motif — "living ink":** the hero wordmark settles like wet ink on paper (SVG `feTurbulence` + `feDisplacementMap`), the portrait floats, an accent line breathes. The site's soul is **ink · paper · print · light.** Any new magic should extend this material identity, not introduce a foreign one.

**Anti-references (avoid):** tech-startup flash — neon, particle fields, purple/blue gradients, dark-mode glow, glassmorphism-everywhere, modal-heavy UIs. Nothing janky or attention-grabbing for its own sake.

**"Wow" calibration references (implicit):** the restrained virtuosity of Linear, Stripe, Vercel, and personal sites by Rauno Freiberg, Emil Kowalski, and Paco Coursey — extraordinary craft that reads as *inevitable*, never loud.

### Design Principles
1. **Inevitable, not loud.** If an effect announces itself as an "effect," it's wrong. Refinement *is* the wow.
2. **Editorial restraint.** Generous space, deliberate type, one confident gesture per view — never competing focal points.
3. **Alive but calm.** Motion should feel like breathing, paper, ink, and light — organic and physical, never frantic. 60fps or don't ship it.
4. **Earn the detail.** Craft lives in the last 20% — easing curves, stagger timing, sub-pixel alignment, optical adjustments. Sweat these.
5. **Accessible by default.** Always honor `prefers-reduced-motion` and `prefers-color-scheme`; keep contrast strong; never amputate functionality on mobile. Every effect must degrade to something still beautiful.

### Tech constraints
Astro 6 (MPA, no React/Vue — components are `.astro` with vanilla `<script>`), deployed on Cloudflare. Pagefind for search; Sanity available. Effects should be framework-light, progressively enhanced, and SSR/MPA-friendly.
