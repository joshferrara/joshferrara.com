Once You're In

Browse and watch anything

That's really it. Open the app, pick something, hit play.

  • Movies & TV. The libraries are organized just like Netflix — browse by collection, search by title, or pick up where you left off.
  • Music. Open Plexamp, sign in, and the whole catalog is yours. It also builds smart radio stations and mixes.
  • Audiobooks. Open Prologue (iOS) or the main Plex app, find a book, and it'll remember your spot across devices.
  • Cross-device. Start something on your phone on the way home and pick it up on the TV when you walk in. It just works.
Don't See Something?

Request what you want to watch

If a movie, show, or season isn't already in the library, request it on the request page. Search for the title, click Request, and it'll usually appear within a short time — no need to ask me directly.
