## Lodging
- **The Cosmopolitan** is our absolutely favorite. It's a little more modern and edgy than some of the others, has incredible food and shopping options, and a variety of pools and spa amenities. It's one of the few hotels with balconies, and a majority of them overlook the Bellagio fountains. It has a smaller gaming floor than most resorts, so consider that if gambling is your main priority.
- **Caesar's Palace** and **The Venetian** are both great "classic Vegas" options. A little more old school, but very nice inside. Great shopping and solid dining on property as well.
- Wynn & Encore are also pretty swanky. They’re further away from the center of the strip than I prefer, but they’re still definitely worth considering.
