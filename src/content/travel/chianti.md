## Lodging
- [Hotel Radda in Chianti](https://www.hotelraddainchianti.it/en) — This was another highlight of our trip. After a few busy days in some more populated European cities, we rented a car and drove here from Venice. This place was a breath of fresh air! Overlooking the wine country of Tuscany, it felt like we had the entire property all to ourselves. Incredible food (although there were also great spots in town), a great spa, and amazing views from our room’s balcony all made this a place we’ll definitely visit again.
