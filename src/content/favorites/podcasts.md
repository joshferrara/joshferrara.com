### My Favorite Podcasts

### Recommendations for your listening pleasure

If you know me very well, you’ve probably learned that I love running errands because it gives me time to pop in my headphones and enjoy a podcast. Between books, articles, and podcasts, I consume a lot of content each week. Podcasts are my favorite content medium, by a long shot.

Of course, podcasts are as diverse as the people listening to them, so this is only a list of my favorites, not necessarily a list anyone else will love.

1. [Reconcilable Differences](https://www.relay.fm/rd) — I absolutely love this show. Two eccentric guys analyzing each other, hoping to learn what makes them tick.
1. [The Talk Show](https://daringfireball.net/thetalkshow/)  — The “two dudes talking” podcast style is common, but John Gruber is one of the originals. An Apple fan, talking about related topics with friends.
1. [Accidental Tech Podcast](http://atp.fm/) — This is what happens when three hyper-analytical guys sit down every week to discuss the world of technology and beyond.
1. [Surprisingly Awesome](https://gimletmedia.com/show/surprisingly-awesome/) — Each week, the two hosts take turns finding a seemingly boring topic, researching it, and then trying to convince their counterpart that it is, in fact, surprisingly awesome.
1. [Lore](http://www.lorepodcast.com/)  — Campfire-style thriller stories, rooted in everyday folklore.
1. [Hello Internet](http://www.hellointernet.fm/) — A show about what’s going on around the internet, by two guys who know it very well.
1. [99% Invisible](http://99percentinvisible.org/)  — A look into the thought that goes into designing the things we encounter everyday.
1. [Criminal](http://thisiscriminal.com/)  — A podcast about crime. Victims, perpetrators, and researchers.
1. [Flash Forward](http://www.flashforwardpod.com/) — A show about possible futures. So much fun.
1. [Radiolab](http://www.radiolab.org/)  — Originally a true radio program, this one is a show about curiosity. High production value makes this one an enjoyable listen.
1. [Slack Variety Pack](https://slack.com/varietypack) — A podcast about work, people, and teams.
1. [Stuff You Should Know](http://www.stuffyoushouldknow.com/podcasts) — Common things and how they work.
1. [The Tim Ferriss Show](http://fourhourworkweek.com/podcast/) — A show about “deconstructing world-class performers”. I always walk away with something to work on.
1. [StartUp](https://gimletmedia.com/show/startup/)  — A show about what it’s really like to start a business.
1. [Serial](https://serialpodcast.org/)  — No list of podcasts is complete without this one; the literal most popular podcast ever. A modern spin on the classic radio mystery show.
1. [Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend](https://www.earwolf.com/show/conan-obrien/) —
1. [Armchair Expert w/ Dax Shephard](https://armchairexpertpod.com/) —
1. [The Daily](https://www.nytimes.com/column/the-daily) — A 20-minute daily podcast from The New York Times. Diving in to current cultural events in quick, bite-sized way.
1. [This Cultural Moment](https://thisculturalmoment.com/) — A podcast about following Jesus in the post-Christian world.
1. [Extremities](https://anchor.fm/extremities) — Why and how people live in earth's most isolated and extreme locations.
